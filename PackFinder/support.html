About PackFinder

PackFinder is a solution to the problem that many cylists face: long, lonely rides in the saddle. PackFinder is not only a tool for finding other cyclists to ride with but also for organizing training schedules and pushing your cycling abilities to the limits. Using PackFinder's scheduling system teams can easily plan their rides and hold each other accountable.

The inspiration for PackFinder comes from countless days facing never ending headwinds without a pack. It is tough for introductory cyclists to get pack experiennce on their own which leads to many of them missing out on the social aspect of cycling.
